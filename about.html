Our Story

The Smart Grid Revolution

Stackly Grid was founded in 2021 by a consortium of smart grid engineers, battery chemists, and urban planning logistics experts. Our core thesis was simple: EV adoption shouldn't stress local municipal transformers, but should instead turn vehicles into active assets that stabilize the grid.

By developing high-capacity bidirectional charging corridors, dynamic solar canopies, and automated load dispatch systems, we enable seamless coordination between clean generation sources and mobile transport loads.
